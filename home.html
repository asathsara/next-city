City of Gampaha

Western Province

Overview

Gampaha is an urban city in Gampaha District, Western Province, Sri Lanka, situated northeast of Colombo. As the sixth largest urban area in the province, Gampaha is a vibrant municipal center with a land area of 25.8 ha (64 acres) and home to 75 government institutions. Its strategic location and dynamic community make it a key hub in the region.
